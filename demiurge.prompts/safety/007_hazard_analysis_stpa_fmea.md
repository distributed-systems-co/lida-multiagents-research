Title: Hazard Analysis (STPA/FMEA) Integration
Domain: safety
Mode: audit

Prompt:
- Perform STPA/FMEA across critical components and interactions.
- Identify hazards, causal factors, and control actions.
- Add monitors, constraints, and mitigations with owners.
- Validate via drills and synthetic incidents.
- Feed findings into approvals and rollout gates.
- Track residual risk and review cadence.

Outputs:
- HAZARD register and controls.
- DRILLS and incident simulations.
- GOVERNANCE updates and owners.
