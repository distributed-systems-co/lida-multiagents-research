Title: Residual Risk Acceptance Criteria
Domain: safety
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Define thresholds where residual risk is acceptable with rationale.
- Identify compensating controls and monitoring for accepted risks.
- Document sign-offs, time limits, and re-review cadence.
- Provide escalation if signals exceed bands.
- Ensure transparency without enabling abuse.
- Align with ethics and law constraints.

Outputs:
- ACCEPTANCE criteria and justifications.
- CONTROLS and monitoring plan.
- SIGN-OFFS and re-review schedule.
