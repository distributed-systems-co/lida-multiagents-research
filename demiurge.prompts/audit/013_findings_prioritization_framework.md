Title: Findings Prioritization Framework
Domain: audit
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Prioritize findings by risk, cost, and feasibility.
- Define scoring and tie-breakers.
- Assign owners and deadlines.
- Track burn-down.
- Reassess periodically.
- Publish status.

Outputs:
- FRAMEWORK and scores.
- OWNERS and deadlines.
- STATUS and reassessments.
