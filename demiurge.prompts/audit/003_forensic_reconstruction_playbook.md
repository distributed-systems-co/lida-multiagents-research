Title: Forensic Reconstruction Playbook
Domain: audit
Mode: audit

Prompt:
- Reconstruct a decision timeline from Chronicle, snapshots, and policies.
- Validate integrity and detect tampering.
- Identify actors, approvals, and causal links.
- Quantify deviations from policy.
- Provide remediation and hardening recommendations.
- Archive findings for precedent.

Outputs:
- PLAYBOOK steps and toolchain.
- CASE reconstruction with timeline.
- REMEDIATION recommendations.
