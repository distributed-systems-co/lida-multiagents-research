Title: Selective Service Shutdowns
Domain: quarantine
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Design targeted shutdowns that minimize collateral damage.
- Define selection criteria, dependencies, and sequencing.
- Preserve evidence and enable read‑only modes.
- Communicate clearly to stakeholders.
- Provide restart tests and health checks.
- Record lessons learned.

Outputs:
- SHUTDOWN playbook and criteria.
- HEALTH checks and restart procedures.
- COMMUNICATION and post‑mortem.
