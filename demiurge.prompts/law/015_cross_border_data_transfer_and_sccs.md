Title: Cross‑Border Data Transfer and SCC Mapping
Domain: law
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Map data flows and identify cross‑border transfers; classify by data type and sensitivity.
- Select appropriate transfer mechanisms (SCCs, BCRs); map clauses to controls.
- Implement technical and organizational measures (TOMs); document proofs.
- Provide DPIAs and risk assessments; consult counsel.
- Build approval workflows and data subject rights handling.
- Publish transparency summaries; update records of processing.
- Audit transfers and vendors; track incidents and remedial actions.
- Review annually and on legal changes.

Outputs:
- FLOW map, SCC/BCR mappings, TOMs.
- DPIA templates and approvals workflow.
- TRANSPARENCY and audit procedures.

Assumptions:
- Data inventories are complete; vendors cooperate.
- Legal counsel engaged; resources available.
- Technical controls enforceable and observable.

Metrics to watch:
- Transfer coverage, audit pass rates, incident counts.
- Data subject request SLAs; vendor compliance.
- Policy update latency on legal changes.
