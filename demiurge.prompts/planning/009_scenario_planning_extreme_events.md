Title: Scenario Planning for Extreme Events
Domain: planning
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Construct contrasting extreme scenarios (best/worst/surprising) with triggers.
- Pre-commit guardrails, freeze points, and decision thresholds per scenario.
- Allocate slack S and R* for each branch; estimate cost-of-delay.
- Define sensing signals and early indicators; attach confidence.
- Prepare branch-specific comms and approvals.
- Schedule exercises and periodic refresh.

Outputs:
- SCENARIO set with triggers and thresholds.
- BRANCH guardrails and slack allocations.
- EXERCISE plan and review cadence.
