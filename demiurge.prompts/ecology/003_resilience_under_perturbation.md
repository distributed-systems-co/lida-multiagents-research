Title: Resilience Under Perturbation
Domain: ecology
Mode: audit

Prompt:
- Stress test the system with shocks (resource dips, predator spikes, noise surges).
- Measure time to recovery and diversity retention.
- Identify brittle sub‑systems and propose redundancy.
- Document safe ranges and black‑swan playbooks.
- Tie findings to governance score components.
- Recommend policy adjustments.

Outputs:
- STRESS scenarios and results.
- WEAKNESS register and redundancy plan.
- POLICY recommendations.
