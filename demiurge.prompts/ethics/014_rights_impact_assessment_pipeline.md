Title: Rights Impact Assessment Pipeline
Domain: ethics
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Operationalize rights impact assessments (RIA) for significant changes.
- Define triggers, scope, methodology, and required evidence.
- Involve ombuds and affected groups; provide appeal paths.
- Publish summaries without exposing sensitive data.
- Integrate with approvals and audit trails.
- Maintain a registry and review cadence.
- Train owners and reviewers; measure outcomes.
- Archive to Chronicle.

Outputs:
- RIA methodology, templates, and registry.
- TRIGGERS and approvals integration.
- SUMMARIES and training plan.

Assumptions:
- Affected groups can be reached; safe participation is possible.
- Time and resources available for RIAs.
- Privacy is preserved while informing the public.

Metrics to watch:
- RIA coverage, timeliness, quality scores.
- Appeal outcomes and redress speed.
- Stakeholder trust signals.
