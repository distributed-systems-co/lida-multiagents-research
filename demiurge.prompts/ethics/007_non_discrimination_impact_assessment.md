Title: Non‑Discrimination Impact Assessment
Domain: ethics
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Identify protected classes and potential disparate impacts.
- Define tests, thresholds, and remediation steps.
- Provide transparency and appeals without re‑identification risks.
- Track improvements and publish summaries.
- Include community consultation where appropriate.
- Align with the rights charter.

Outputs:
- IMPACT assessment report.
- TESTS and remediation plan.
- TRANSPARENCY summaries and cadence.
