Title: Consent and Opt‑Out Design
Domain: ethics
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Create informed consent templates for experiments/interventions.
- Design reversible opt‑out with minimal friction.
- Clarify data usage, retention, and privacy terms plainly.
- Provide special handling for minors and proxies.
- Include transparency reports and subject access requests.
- Add non‑coercion safeguards and time‑limited prompts.

Outputs:
- CONSENT templates and UX flows.
- PRIVACY and data policy summaries.
- REPORTING plan and subject tools.
