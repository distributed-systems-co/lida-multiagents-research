Title: Fairness, Accountability, Transparency (FAT) Profile
Domain: ethics
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Define fairness criteria and tests for the intervention.
- Document accountability: owners, approvals, and appeal routes.
- Specify transparency: what, to whom, when.
- Address disparate impact and remedies.
- Provide monitoring and redress mechanisms.
- Align with rights charter and ombuds independence.

Outputs:
- FAT profile document.
- TESTS and monitoring plan.
- REMEDIES and escalation paths.
