Title: Trace Decisions to Metrics and Laws
Domain: explain
Mode: audit

Prompt:
- Produce an explicit trace from decision → metrics → laws → constraints.
- Identify each invariant or policy touched and compliance status.
- Compute expected metric deltas and uncertainty bands.
- Provide a compact dependency graph diagram (ASCII acceptable).
- List approvals actually used and their timestamps.
- Summarize auditability and remaining gaps.

Outputs:
- TRACE document and dependency diagram.
- METRIC deltas and CI table.
- COMPLIANCE checklist with approvals evidence.
