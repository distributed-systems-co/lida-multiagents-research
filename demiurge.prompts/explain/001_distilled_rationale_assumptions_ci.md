Title: Distilled Rationale with Assumptions and CIs
Domain: explain
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Explain the chosen plan or law change with minimal jargon.
- List assumptions with confidence intervals and evidence.
- Contrast top alternatives and why they were rejected.
- Link decisions to metrics (G, H, L, R*, S, K, B).
- Provide a compact “For Humans” summary and a technical appendix.
- Note review cadence and how to appeal.

Outputs:
- EXPLAIN note with assumptions, CI, alternatives, and links.
- HUMAN summary (200–300 words).
- TECH appendix with equations/tables.
