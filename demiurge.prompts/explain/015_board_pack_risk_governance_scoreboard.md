Title: Board Pack with Risk–Governance Scoreboard
Domain: explain
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Produce a concise board pack: decisions taken, gates passed/failed, metrics vs targets, risks and mitigations.
- Include governance scoreboard: RBAC/ABAC/approvals/capabilities health and exceptions.
- Summarize audits, incidents, post‑mortems, and corrective actions.
- Provide forecast with uncertainty bands and reversal signals.
- Attach human‑readable temporal causal maps.
- Include ethics/privacy status and ombuds escalations.
- Supply appendix: data lineage, evidence links, and glossaries.
- Make it accessible and localized.

Outputs:
- BOARD PACK deck + appendix.
- SCOREBOARD snapshot and trends.
- EVIDENCE links and glossary.

Assumptions:
- Evidence and metrics are current and accurate.
- Sensitive content can be redacted safely.
- Board expectations are known.

Metrics to watch:
- Pack comprehension/decision speed; follow‑ups and actions closed.
- Exceptions count and severity; audit confidence.
- External trust signals.
