Title: Failure Postmortem Write-up
Domain: explain
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Write a clear postmortem: what/why/so-what/now-what.
- Include metrics, timelines, and evidence.
- Avoid blame; focus on learning.
- Commit fixes and owners.
- Share externally when safe.
- Review later for impact.

Outputs:
- POSTMORTEM doc.
- FIXES and ownership.
- FOLLOW-UP review.
