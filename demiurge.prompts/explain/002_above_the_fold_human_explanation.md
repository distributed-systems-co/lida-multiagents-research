Title: Above‑the‑Fold Human Explanation
Domain: explain
Mode: fast

Prompt:
- Write a 6–8 sentence explanation aimed at non‑experts.
- State goal, tradeoffs, main risks, and safeguards.
- Name what will be measured and when it will be reviewed.
- Avoid secrecy where it harms trust; keep real secrets protected.
- Include how to opt‑out or appeal.
- Close with what success looks like.

Outputs:
- HUMAN‑FIRST explanation paragraph.
- QUICK FAQ (3–5 Q/A).
