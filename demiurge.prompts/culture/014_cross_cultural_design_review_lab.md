Title: Cross‑Cultural Design Review and Localization Lab
Domain: culture
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Establish a review lab for cross‑cultural design; include community panels and localization experts.
- Test artifacts for clarity, respect, and inclusion; document findings.
- Provide localization glossaries and governance; avoid erasure.
- Ensure accessibility standards; measure comprehension and satisfaction.
- Publish guidance and case studies; update regularly.
- Integrate with approvals and release gates.
- Archive decisions and rationales to Chronicle.
- Track longitudinal outcomes.

Outputs:
- REVIEW lab charter and process.
- GLOSSARY governance and guidelines.
- REPORTS and updates.

Assumptions:
- Community participation is safe and resourced.
- Localization infra exists; a11y is enforceable.
- Team buy‑in for review gates.

Metrics to watch:
- Comprehension scores and satisfaction by culture.
- Localization errors, a11y defects; fix time.
- Appeal rates and remedy outcomes.
