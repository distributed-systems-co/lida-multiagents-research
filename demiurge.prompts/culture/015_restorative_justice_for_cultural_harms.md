Title: Restorative Justice for Cultural Harms
Domain: culture
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Define a restorative justice process for cultural harms (acknowledge, repair, prevent).
- Provide safe reporting, triage, and mediation with community participation.
- Offer remedies (apologies, changes, restitution) and track follow‑through.
- Publish transparent summaries without re‑traumatization.
- Integrate with policy updates and training.
- Audit outcomes with independent oversight.
- Archive anonymized precedents.
- Measure trust and effectiveness.

Outputs:
- PROCESS and governance.
- REMEDIES catalog and tracking.
- REPORTS, audits, and training.

Assumptions:
- Psychological safety and support exist for participants.
- Independence of oversight is credible.
- Data is handled ethically.

Metrics to watch:
- Case resolution time and satisfaction.
- Recurrence of harms; policy updates performed.
- Trust surveys and participation rates.
