Title: Trial‑to‑Paid and Activation Optimization
Domain: economy
Mode: fast

Prompt:
- Map activation milestones and value moments; reduce time‑to‑value.
- Test onboarding flows, nudges, and triggers.
- Enforce ethical UX (no dark patterns) and clear consent.
- Measure activation, conversion, and margin.
- Add saves and win‑backs.
- Share quick wins.

Outputs:
- ACTIVATION plan and tests.
- KPI tracker and quick wins.
- ETHICAL UX checklist.

Assumptions:
- Value moments are identifiable and can be delivered quickly.
- Email/push channels comply with consent and frequency caps.
- Engineering/design capacity exists for iterative experiments.

Metrics to watch:
- Activation %, time‑to‑value, trial‑to‑paid %, cohort retention.
- Customer satisfaction, complaints, unsubscribes.
- Support load, edge‑case failures, and regressions.
