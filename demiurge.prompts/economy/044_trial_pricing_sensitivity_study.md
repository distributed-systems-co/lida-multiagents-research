Title: Trial Pricing Sensitivity Study
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Explore sensitivity to trial pricing (deposit, token, $0 with limits) by segment.
- Control for confounders; ensure fairness and avoid exclusionary effects.
- Measure retention, conversion, ARPU, margin, and support load.
- Build decision rules for adopting trial pricing.
- Provide comms and policy updates.
- Ensure compliance and transparency.
- Archive outcomes.
- Define re‑test cadence.

Outputs:
- STUDY design and segments.
- RESULTS with CI and recommendations.
- POLICY/comms updates and next tests.

Assumptions:
- Technical ability to implement trial variants.
- Sufficient traffic and tolerance for experimentation.
- Legal review of pricing practices completed.

Metrics to watch:
- Conversion/retention impacts, ARPU, margin, support contacts.
- Complaint rates and fairness indicators.
- Long‑term cohort effects.
