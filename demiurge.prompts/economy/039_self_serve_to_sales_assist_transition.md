Title: Self‑Serve to Sales‑Assist Transition Design
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Define triggers (ACV, complexity) to shift from self‑serve to sales‑assist.
- Build playbooks for inbound qualification and assist motions.
- Price adjustments and value communication without bait‑and‑switch.
- Instrument handoffs and SLA compliance.
- Measure effect on conversion, margin, and CSAT.
- Train teams and iterate scripts.
- Ensure fairness and transparency in offers.
- Publish internal docs and guardrails.

Outputs:
- TRIGGERS and playbooks.
- SLA instrumentation and dashboards.
- TRAINING and guardrails.

Assumptions:
- Tooling supports clean handoffs and tracking.
- Messaging and pricing are aligned across motions.
- Teams have capacity for assist without starving self‑serve.

Metrics to watch:
- Conversion lift, ACV changes, margin impact.
- SLA adherence, CSAT, complaint signals.
- Time‑in‑stage and pipeline velocity.
