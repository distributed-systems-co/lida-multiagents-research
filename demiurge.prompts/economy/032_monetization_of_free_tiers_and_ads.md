Title: Monetization of Free Tiers and Ads
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Design free‑tier monetization via limits, add‑ons, or ethical ads.
- Avoid degrading user trust; provide clear upgrade paths.
- Balance ad load vs retention and quality.
- Test offers, prices, and targeting under privacy constraints.
- Measure ARPU, margin, and LTV impact.
- Publish policy and controls.

Outputs:
- FREE‑TIER monetization design.
- TEST plan and results.
- POLICY and controls.

Assumptions:
- Free users derive clear value; monetization does not coerce or mislead.
- Ads (if any) respect privacy, relevance, and frequency limits.
- Technical enforcement of limits and abuse prevention is feasible.

Metrics to watch:
- Free→paid conversion, ARPU uplift, retention changes.
- Ad revenue per user (if applicable), quality complaints, CTR quality.
- Abuse/fraud signals and support tickets.
