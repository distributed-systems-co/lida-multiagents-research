Title: SaaS Renewals and Expansion Revenue
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Build renewal/expansion playbooks (QBRs, usage/value reviews, success plans).
- Design price increases with fairness and notice; prevent churn shocks.
- Add seat/feature expansion paths; test uplift.
- Define health scores and risk alerts.
- Measure NRR/GRR and margin.
- Publish governance and outcomes.

Outputs:
- RENEWAL/EXPANSION playbooks.
- PRICE policy and fairness rules.
- NRR/GRR dashboards and reviews.

Assumptions:
- Clear value documentation exists to support price changes and expansions.
- Customer success has coverage and playbook adherence.
- Legal/compliance guardrails for renewals and notices are integrated.

Metrics to watch:
- NRR/GRR, expansion %, contraction %, logo churn.
- Seat/feature adoption, health scores, risk alerts.
- Ticket volume, escalation, and customer satisfaction.
