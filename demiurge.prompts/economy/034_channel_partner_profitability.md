Title: Channel Partner Profitability and Tiering
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Model partner-sourced profitability by tier/geo/product; include MDF, enablement, and rev-share costs.
- Define tiering (silver/gold/platinum) with requirements, benefits, and graduation/demotion rules.
- Design conflict resolution (direct vs partner) and attribution windows.
- Propose enablement curriculum and certification impacts on performance.
- Add partner scorecards and health reviews; set remediation paths.
- Ensure ethics and fairness; avoid predatory channel behavior.
- Plan launch, communications, and contract templates.
- Set review cadence and exit criteria for non-performing partners.

Outputs:
- TIERING framework, scorecards, and rev-share tables.
- ENABLEMENT plan and certification ladder.
- CONFLICT and attribution policy with examples.

Assumptions:
- Data sharing agreements and partner reporting are feasible.
- Legal constraints on incentives and exclusivity are respected.
- Internal capacity exists for partner management at scale.

Metrics to watch:
- Partner-sourced revenue, margin, pipeline velocity, certification rates.
- Channel conflict incidents, clawbacks, compliance findings.
- Partner churn, satisfaction, and net contribution.
