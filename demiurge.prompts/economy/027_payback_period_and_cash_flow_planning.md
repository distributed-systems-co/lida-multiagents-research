Title: Payback Period and Cash Flow Planning
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Build payback curves by channel/segment; set thresholds for scale.
- Plan growth subject to cash and runway constraints.
- Sequence spend by payback and risk.
- Add credit/collections policies to improve cash conversion.
- Provide dashboards and alerts.
- Review monthly; adjust quickly.

Outputs:
- PAYBACK curves and policies.
- SPEND plan under runway constraints.
- DASHBOARDS and alerts.

Assumptions:
- Collection cycles and payment terms are modeled realistically.
- Spend pacing can be adjusted within governance limits.
- Forecast error is tracked and incorporated.

Metrics to watch:
- Payback by cohort/channel, cash conversion cycle, burn/runway.
- Plan vs actual on spend and revenue; alert hit rates.
- Risk flags: delinquency, churn spikes, unit cost drift.
