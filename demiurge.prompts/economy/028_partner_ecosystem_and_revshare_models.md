Title: Partner Ecosystem and Rev‑Share Models
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Design partner programs (affiliate, reseller, integration) with rev‑share models.
- Prevent cannibalization; define attribution and channel conflict policy.
- Set qualification tiers, enablement, and MBOs.
- Model partner economics and risk.
- Publish program docs and contracts.
- Plan launch and measurement.

Outputs:
- PROGRAM design and rev‑share.
- POLICY on conflict/attribution.
- LAUNCH and measurement plan.

Assumptions:
- Contracts, attribution windows, and data sharing are feasible and lawful.
- Channel conflict resolution and tiering rules are enforceable.
- Partner enablement can deliver expected outcomes.

Metrics to watch:
- Partner‑sourced revenue, gross margin, CAC via partner, pipeline velocity.
- Conflict incidents, clawbacks, compliance findings.
- Enablement engagement and certification rates.
