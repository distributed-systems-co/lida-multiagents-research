Title: SKU Rationalization and Long‑Tail Policy
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Analyze SKU profitability; flag long‑tail with low margin/throughput.
- Propose retire/merge/preserve actions with customer impact analysis.
- Provide substitution/transition offers; ensure fairness.
- Plan communications and deprecation timelines.
- Track portfolio margin uplift and support load changes.
- Archive rationale to the Chronicle.
- Add governance for future SKU additions.
- Align with brand and regulatory constraints.

Outputs:
- SKU actions list with rationale.
- TRANSITION plans and comms.
- PORTFOLIO metrics and governance.

Assumptions:
- Reliable cost and demand signals per SKU.
- Ops can execute transitions without high risk.
- Legal/brand approvals are secured.

Metrics to watch:
- Margin uplift, mix shift, customer satisfaction, churn.
- Support tickets, returns, logistics complexity.
- New SKU acceptance and performance.
