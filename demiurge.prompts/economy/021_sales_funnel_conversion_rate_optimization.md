Title: Sales Funnel Conversion Optimization
Domain: economy
Mode: fast

Prompt:
- Instrument the funnel (impressions→clicks→leads→SQL→win) with drop‑off analysis.
- Prioritize friction removal and message/product fit fixes.
- Design A/Bs for steps with highest expected impact.
- Add SLAs and enablement for handoffs.
- Track win rates, cycle lengths, and blended CAC.
- Share quick wins and long‑term fixes.

Outputs:
- FUNNEL map and drop‑off table.
- EXPERIMENT plan and enablement.
- KPI tracker and quick wins list.

Assumptions:
- Funnel instrumentation is trustworthy; sampling windows are stable.
- Experiment velocity can be maintained without quality degradation.
- Sales/CS alignment exists for handoffs and SLAs.

Metrics to watch:
- Step conversion %, cycle time, win rate, blended CAC.
- Lead quality, qualification fidelity, and SLA adherence.
- Customer feedback on friction and clarity.
