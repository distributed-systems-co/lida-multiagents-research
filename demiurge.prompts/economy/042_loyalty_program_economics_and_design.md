Title: Loyalty Program Economics and Design
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Design loyalty tiers/rewards aligned with profit (breakage, liability, cadence).
- Prevent abuse; add fraud controls and fairness rules.
- Test earn/burn rates and partner tie‑ins; simulate balance sheet impact.
- Provide redemption UX and communication plan.
- Measure incrementality vs control and long‑term retention.
- Ensure regulatory/tax compliance for rewards.
- Publish program docs and deprecation process.
- Plan review cadence and changes.

Outputs:
- PROGRAM spec and economics model.
- FRAUD controls and audit plan.
- INCREMENTALITY results and roadmap.

Assumptions:
- Accounting treatment and tax implications are defined.
- Fraud tooling exists; partners are vetted.
- Sufficient traffic for robust tests.

Metrics to watch:
- LTV uplift, redemption rates, liability exposure, breakage.
- Fraud incidents, abuse patterns, CSAT.
- Partner contribution and satisfaction.
