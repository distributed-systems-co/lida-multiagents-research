Title: Geo Expansion Profitability and Playbook
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Prioritize geos via TAM, ease of entry, regulation, and cost structures.
- Build launch P&L: localization, taxes/duties, channels, staffing, SLAs.
- Define pricing/packaging per geo; account for willingness‑to‑pay and fairness.
- Create playbook for marketing, sales, support, and partnerships.
- Establish compliance and data residency measures.
- Set exit criteria for underperforming geos.
- Plan measurement and governance.
- Stage rollouts with milestones.

Outputs:
- GEO short‑list and P&L models.
- PLAYBOOK and compliance checklist.
- STAGED rollout and exit criteria.

Assumptions:
- Sufficient market research; local partners available.
- Cost and regulatory estimates within tolerances.
- Org readiness for localization and support.

Metrics to watch:
- Revenue/margin vs plan, CAC/payback, NPS.
- Compliance findings, latency/quality metrics.
- Hiring/onboarding speed and partner performance.
