Title: Promo ROI Measurement and Holdouts
Domain: economy
Mode: audit

Prompt:
- Define ROI measurement for promos with matched holdouts and ghost variants.
- Attribute lift vs pull‑forward; measure margin after all costs.
- Detect cannibalization; set stop rules.
- Publish dashboards and post‑promo reviews.
- Store learnings in a playbook.
- Update policy accordingly.

Outputs:
- ROI measurement framework.
- DASHBOARDS and post‑promo template.
- POLICY updates and stop rules.

Assumptions:
- Ability to run matched holdouts/ghost promos without spillovers.
- All‑in cost accounting (COGS, logistics, support) is available.
- Promo calendar avoids interference with major events.

Metrics to watch:
- Incremental profit vs control; cannibalization share; pull‑forward lag.
- Refund/return rates, support load, complaint volume.
- Playbook reuse rate and sustained lift beyond promo window.
