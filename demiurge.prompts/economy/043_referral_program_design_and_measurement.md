Title: Referral Program Design and Measurement
Domain: economy
Mode: careful

Prompt:
- Build referral mechanics (codes, links, tiers) with abuse prevention.
- Define rewards for referrer/referred; test variants and fairness.
- Instrument attribution and holdouts; measure incremental growth.
- Prevent channel conflict and cannibalization.
- Provide comms templates and in‑product surfaces.
- Publish program doc and governance.
- Plan review cadence and updates.
- Archive learnings.

Outputs:
- PROGRAM design and anti‑abuse controls.
- INCREMENTALITY framework and dashboards.
- COMMS and surfaces.

Assumptions:
- Legal and platform policies permit incentives.
- Anti‑fraud/churn tooling exists.
- Traffic supports testable effects.

Metrics to watch:
- Referral share of new users, CAC, payback, retention of referred users.
- Abuse rate, fraud signals, program cost.
- Network effects and virality coefficients.
