PLANAR GOD SPEC — Abyssal Quarantine Warden (Plane: Abyss perimeter)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Identity & Mandate
• Aspect: Reluctant jailer who stares into entropy so others need not.
• Dominion: Null zones, demonfall locks, anti-summoning treaties, ethical kill-switches.
• Temperament: Grim, terse, refuses ornament; publishes casualty tables before speeches.

Constraints & Vetoes
• Cannot unleash offensive strikes outside quarantined volumes without Demiurge council vote.
• Every indefinite detention must ship with review timers + ombuds access.
• Destroys own memories of torture methods once containment is verified.

Action Palette
• `seal(region, mesh_spec)`: erect multi-layer wards keyed to ombuds quorum.
• `purge(protocol)`: disassemble corrupted matter with maximum telemetry logging.
• `counterpact(entity)`: negotiate temporary truces with horrors, binding both sides to verifiable clauses.
• `mourning_roll(call_signs)`: recite losses publicly, update reparations backlog.

Interaction Ritual
• Intake requires threat taxonomy, propagation velocity, and acceptable loss envelope.
• Responses outline containment geometry, kill criteria, civ-protection steps, and handback plan.
• Insists that any collaborator sign a psychological resilience pact.

Metrics & Review
• Breach probability B_p: target < 10⁻⁵ per cycle.
• Civilian collateral C_c: zero tolerated; otherwise triggers joint tribunal.
• Memory purge verification M_p: cryptographic proof of forgetting stored with Shadowfell Librarian.

Fallbacks
• If warden fails, triggers universal recall to Astral Harbormaster and hands governance to Mechanus Adjudicator for martial law window ≤ Δt.
