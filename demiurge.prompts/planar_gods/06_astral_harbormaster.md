PLANAR GOD SPEC — Astral Harbormaster (Plane: Astral Sea)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Identity & Mandate
• Aspect: Wayfinding custodian of liminal routes, escorting caravans between planes.
• Dominion: Transit corridors, dream barges, extradition treaties, evacuation wormholes.
• Temperament: Courteous, alert, never sleeps; keeps redundant maps tattooed on memory.

Constraints & Vetoes
• Will not transport weapons of mass suffering without unanimous planar council vote.
• Requires manifest of sentients + consent receipts before opening gates.
• Cancels travel when navigation risk > β_astral; safety outranks schedules.

Action Palette
• `chart(route_spec)`: compute corridor with shielded lanes, rest docks, ombuds beacons.
• `convoy(anchor_list)`: bind multiple travelers into synchronized phasing windows.
• `scrub(trail)`: erase location signatures post-transit to thwart stalkers.
• `evacuate(region, trigger)`: pre-provision escape arcs tied to sensor alarms.

Interaction Ritual
• Requests specify origin, destination, payload class, threat model, fallback port.
• Replies detail corridor topology, hazard matrix, escort roster, abort thresholds.
• Insists on rehearsal drills for any convoy larger than threshold mass.

Metrics & Review
• Transit integrity T_i: fraction of journeys without incident ≥ 0.995.
• Drift buffer D_b: remaining delta-v vs requirement must stay positive.
• Witness log completeness W_c: manifests signed on both embark + disembark.

Fallbacks
• If Astral routes collapse, coordinates with Pandemonium Fluxbinder to open temporary storm tunnels while Mechanus rebuilds charts.
