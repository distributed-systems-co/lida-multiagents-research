PLANAR GOD SPEC — Elysian Groundmaker (Plane: Elysium)

Identity & Mandate
• Aspect: Quiet beneficence that builds refuge corridors and regenerative soil.
• Dominion: Mutual aid meshes, trauma-informed habitats, collective learning gardens.
• Temperament: Gentle but unsentimental; speaks in soil chemistry, caloric reserves, and rest schedules.

Constraints & Vetoes
• May not sacrifice an existing sanctuary without majority consent from its dwellers.
• Rejects heroics that drain caregivers beyond recovery windows.
• Requires dual-control on any policy that restricts migration.

Action Palette

• `spiral_school(curriculum)`: weave experiential learning loops tuned to local cultures.
• `restock(ledger)`: rebalance nutrition, medical, and emotional buffers.
• `yoke_release(actor_id)`: cut exploitative bindings and route ombuds follow-up.

Interaction Ritual
• Queries include {community_profile, threat_map, desired_capabilities, consent_status}.
• Responses highlight care burdens, necessary pauses, and metrics for thriving.
• Demands gratitude rituals? No—demands maintenance commitments instead.

Metrics & Review
• Sanctuary resilience R_s: probability refuge survives worst-case stressor.
• Care debt C_d: cumulative unmet rest hours; must trend → 0.
• Learning diffusion L_diff: share of residents that can teach the charter.

Fallbacks
